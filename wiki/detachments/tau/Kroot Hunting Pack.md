# Kroot Hunting Pack

**Kroot Hunting Pack** — детачмент фракции T'au Empire, посвящённый исключительно войскам Kroot. Фокусируется на скорости, скрытности и ближнем бое.

## Detachment Rule: Hunting Pack

Kroot units from your army gain the following benefits:
- Add 1 to Advance and Charge rolls.
- Each time a Kroot model makes a melee attack, add 1 to the Strength characteristic of that attack.

## Enhancements

- **Predator's Instincts** (15 pts) — Kroot Character only. While this model is leading a unit, that unit can ignore any or all modifiers to its Advance and Charge rolls.
- **Nomadic Hunter** (10 pts) — Kroot Character only. At the start of the battle round, select one objective marker outside your deployment zone. While a friendly Kroot unit is within range, it can ignore OC modifiers.
- **Alpha Leader** (20 pts) — Kroot Character only. Friendly Kroot units within 6" re-roll Hit rolls of 1.
- **Stealthy Stalker** (25 pts) — Kroot Character only. The bearer's unit can shoot and charge after Advancing.

## Stratagems

| Stratagem | CP | When | Effect |
|-----------|----|------|--------|
| **Feasting on Fear** | 1CP | Fight phase, after a Kroot unit fights | If any enemy models were destroyed, that unit can fight again |
| **Pack Hunters** | 1CP | Your Shooting or Fight phase | One Kroot unit improves AP by 1 until end of phase |
| **Lurking in the Shadows** | 1CP | Opponent's Shooting phase, after target is selected | One Kroot unit gains 5+ Feel No Pain against ranged attacks |
| **Rapid Regroup** | 1CP | Your Command phase | One Battle-shocked Kroot unit is no longer Battle-shocked |
| **Unbound Ferocity** | 1CP | Your Charge phase | One Kroot unit can charge even if it Advanced |
