## Abilities

- **For the Greater Good (FTGG)**
- **Markerlight**
- **Invocations:** This model knows two Invocations from the following list and can use *both* of them in your Command phase (other Ethereals may only use one):
  - *Power of Tides* (Reroll Hit rolls of 1 for a friendly T'au Empire unit within 12")
  - *Sense of Stone* (Feel No Pain 5+ against mortal wounds for a friendly T'au Empire unit within 12")
  - *Storm of Fire* (Reroll Wound rolls of 1 for a friendly T'au Empire unit within 12")
- **Master of the Undying Spirit (Aura):** The first time this model is destroyed, roll one D6 at the end of the phase. On a 3+, remove it from the battlefield — it is not considered destroyed. At the start of your next Command phase, you can set this model back up on the battlefield as close as possible to its previous position (and more than 1" away from enemy models) with its full wounds remaining. While this model is not on the battlefield due to this ability, it can still use its Invocations ability during your Command phase.
