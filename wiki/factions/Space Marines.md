# Space Marines

> *"Even in death, I still serve."*

Space Marines (Adeptus Astartes) in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition are the poster-child faction of the game — genetically-enhanced superhumans clad in power armour, organised into Chapters of 1,000 warriors. A versatile elite army with the broadest model range in the game, covering every battlefield role.

## Army Rule: Oath of Moment

At the start of each battle round, select one enemy unit. Your Space Marines army gains full rerolls to hit and full rerolls to wound against that unit for the rest of the battle round.
