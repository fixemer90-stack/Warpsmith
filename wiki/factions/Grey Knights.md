# Grey Knights

> *"I am the hammer. I am the sword and shield of the Emperor. I am the watcher at the gate."*

The Grey Knights in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition are the secret Chapter of psyker Space Marines dedicated to hunting daemons. An elite, highly-mobile army specialising in teleportation assaults, psychic powers, and anti-daemon warfare.

## Army Rule: Teleport Assault

At the end of your opponent's turn, you can pick up to three Grey Knights units on the battlefield and set them up in deep-strike reserves. At the end of your turn, you can deep-strike these units back down anywhere on the board more than 9" from enemies. This grants unparalleled board control and repositioning.
