## Drones

**Guardian Drone:** While this model is equipped with a Guardian Drone, the unit it is transporting has a 4+ Invulnerable save against ranged attacks.

## Abilities

**For The Greater Good (Guided Fire):** When this unit is selected to shoot, if it has a Markerlight token or is within range of a unit with the Markerlight keyword, it can be Guided to re-roll Hit rolls of 1.

**Transport:** This model can transport up to **12 T'AU EMPIRE INFANTRY** models. Each FLY model takes the space of 2 models, and each BATTLESUIT model takes the space of 3 models.

**Firing Deck 12:** Up to 12 models being transported can shoot in the Shooting phase, measuring and drawing line of sight from this model's deck or hatches.

**Explodes:** When this model is destroyed, roll one D6 before removing it from play. On a 6, it explodes and each unit within 6" suffers D3 mortal wounds.
