# Adeptus Custodes

> *"We are the shield of the Emperor."*

The Adeptus Custodes in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition are the genetically-enhanced elite bodyguards of the Emperor Himself. An extremely low-model-count, high-durability army with 4++ invulnerable saves across the board, powerful melee, and tactical flexibility through stance-switching.

## Army Rule: Martial Ka'tah

At the start of each battle round, choose one Ka'tah stance to be active (e.g., Rendax for +1AP in melee, Kaptaris for fights-first, Dacatarai for extra attacks). Some units switch stances mid-round based on conditions.
