# X-101

> *"A cybernetic enforcer built from salvaged battle-automata parts, X-101 serves as an unflinching guardian to its Tech-Priordinator."*

X-101 is a unique Servitor bodyguard, a relic of a bygone era of Martian engineering. It functions as an Epic Hero but does not take up a Leader slot — instead, it joins a Tech-Priest's unit at deployment.

## Servitor Bodyguard

When you declare a unit that contains a Tech-Priest model as a unit making a Leader attachment at the start of the Declare Battle Formations step, this model can join that unit. If it does, this model is attached to that unit and is not counted towards the limit of one Character model per unit.

## Abilities

### Faction: Doctrina Imperatives
This model benefits from the Doctrina Imperatives army rule.

### Mindlock
While this model is within 6\" of a friendly Tech-Priest model, add 1 to the Ballistic Skill and Weapon Skill characteristics of this model's weapons.

### Bound Creation
While a friendly Tech-Priest model is within 3\" of this model, that model has a 5+ Feel No Pain.
