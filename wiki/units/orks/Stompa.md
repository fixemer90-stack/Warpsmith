## Способности (Abilities)

- **Waaagh!:** While this model is within 6" of a friendly **WARLORD** with the **WAAAGH!** ability, add 1 to the Strength characteristic of its melee weapons.
- **Dead Chassis:** Each time an attack is allocated to this model, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack (to a minimum of 1).
- **Titanic:** This model can move over enemy models (but not enemy TITANIC models) as if they were not there. It can move over terrain features as if they were not there. It cannot be pushed within Engagement Range of any enemy TITANIC model.
- **Explodes (6" | D6 mortals):** When this model is destroyed, roll one D6: on a 6, it explodes. Each unit within 6" suffers D6 mortal wounds.
- **Transport 10 ORKS INFANTRY:** This model has a transport capacity of 10 **ORKS INFANTRY** models.
- **Firing Deck 10:** Up to 10 models embarked on this model can shoot from it, measuring from any point on this model.
