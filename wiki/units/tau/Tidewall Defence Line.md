## Abilities

**Fortification:** This model is deployed before the battle begins and cannot move for any reason.

**Cover (Aura):** While a T'AU EMPIRE INFANTRY unit is entirely behind this model, that unit has the Benefit of Cover.

**Transport:** This model has a transport capacity of 6 **T'AU INFANTRY** models (before repairs).

**Explodes:** When this model is destroyed, roll one D6: on a 6, it explodes and each unit within 3" suffers D3 mortal wounds.

## Notes

- The Tidewall Defence Line is a deployable fortification providing mobile cover for T'au Infantry.
- It can be embarked by Strike Teams, Breacher Teams, Pathfinder Teams, and other T'AU INFANTRY.
- Often transported or deployed alongside a [[Devilfish]].
