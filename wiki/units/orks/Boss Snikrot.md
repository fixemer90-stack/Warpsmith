## Способности (Abilities)

**Sneaky Gitz:** Once per battle round, when an enemy unit declares a charge against this unit, if this unit is [[Kommandos|Infantry]] and is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, this unit can make a Normal Move of up to D6". If it does, the charge is resolved as if the charge roll was lower than the distance moved (i.e. the charge fails unless the moving unit can still reach).

**From Da Shadows:** While this model is leading a unit, once per battle, at the start of the first battle round, if this model's unit is in Reserves, you can redeploy it — set it up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 9" away from any enemy models. If the [[Warboss]] is your Warlord and the Waaagh! is declared, this unit can also redeploy at the start of any of your Command phases instead.

## Предводитель (Leader)

This model can be attached to the following unit:
- [[Kommandos]]

When attached, this model gains the **Kommandos** keyword and can use the **Sneaky Gitz** ability for the combined unit.
