# Imperial Knights

> *"Honour in iron. Strength in unity."*

Imperial Knights in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition are the noble-piloted Knight Walkers — towering war-machines smaller than Titans but vastly larger than Space Marine Dreadnoughts. A low-model-count army (typically 3–8 models) where each Knight is a self-contained, heavily-armed threat.

## Army Rule: Oaths of the Noble Houses

Pick one of three Oaths at the start of the battle:
- **Protect the Realm (Hekaton)** — Objective Secured for all Knights
- **Guard the Throne (Aegis)** — Feel No Pain 5+ against mortal wounds
- **Lay Low the Tyrants** — Full rerolls to hit against monsters and vehicles
