# More Dakka!

Стрелковый детачмент с упором на дальний бой и огневую мощь.

## Detachment Rule: Dakka! Dakka! Dakka!

- Ranged weapons equipped by **Orks Infantry** and **Orks Walker** models from your army have the [ASSAULT] ability.
- While the **Waaagh!** is active for your army, during your Shooting phase, ranged weapons equipped by **Orks Infantry** and **Orks Walker** models from your army have the [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability.

## Enhancements

- **Da Gobshot Thunderbuss** (Orks model only) — Ranged weapons equipped by the bearer have the [DEVASTATING WOUNDS] and [HAZARDOUS] abilities.
- **Dead Shiny Shootas** (Orks model only) — Ranged weapons equipped by models in the bearer's unit have the [RAPID FIRE 1] ability.
- **Targetin' Squigs** (Orks model only) — Each time a model in the bearer's unit makes a ranged attack, add 1 to the Hit roll.
- **Zog Off and Eat Dakka!** (Orks model only) — The bearer's unit is eligible to shoot in a turn in which it Fell Back.
