# Blitz Brigade

Штурмовой детачмент с упором на десант из транспортов.

## Detachment Rule: Eager for the Fight

Each time an Orks unit from your army disembarks from a Transport, until the end of the turn, you can re-roll Advance and Charge rolls made for that Orks unit.

## Enhancements

- **Runnin' Boots** (Orks Infantry Character only) — Each time you make a Charge roll for the bearer's unit, if that unit disembarked from a Transport this turn, add 1 to the result.
- **Blitzkaptin** (Orks Character only) — After both players have deployed their armies, if the bearer's unit (or any Transport it is embarked within) is on the battlefield, select up to three Orks Vehicle units from your army and redeploy them. When doing so, you can set those units up in Strategic Reserves, regardless of how many units are already in Strategic Reserves.
- **Supercharged Squig Oil** (Mek only) — Each time the bearer uses its Mekaniak ability, until the end of the turn, you can re-roll Charge rolls for the selected Vehicle model's unit.
- **Tuff Git** (Orks Infantry Character only) — At the end of a phase in which the bearer's unit disembarked from a Transport, if that unit is Battle-shocked, it is no longer Battle-shocked.
