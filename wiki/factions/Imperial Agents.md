# Imperial Agents

> *"The Inquisition watches. The Assassinorum strikes. The Imperial Navy protects."*

Imperial Agents in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition is the catch-all faction for the Imperium's covert, inquisitorial, and auxiliary organisations: the Inquisition, Officio Assassinorum, Imperial Navy, Rogue Traders, Fleet Auxiliaries, and more. Rather than a standalone army, Agents are typically taken as allied detachments or embedded operatives in other Imperial armies.

## Army Rule: Agents of the Imperium

Imperial Agents units can be included in other Imperial armies without breaking army rules. Certain characters (Inquisitors, Vindicare Assassins) can attach to non-Agent units. A full Agents-only army uses special detachment rules.
