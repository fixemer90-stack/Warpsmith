# Astra Militarum

> *"For the Emperor! Fix bayonets!"*

The Astra Militarum (Imperial Guard) in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition is the Imperium's primary military force — countless billions of ordinary humans wielding lasguns, tanks, and artillery. A horde army focused on combined arms: massed infantry backed by Leman Russ battle tanks, Basilisks, and orders from commanding officers.

## Army Rule: Born Soldiers

- When a unit remains stationary, its ranged attacks have **Lethal Hits**
- When a unit receives an order from an Officer model, it gains **Sustained Hits 1**
