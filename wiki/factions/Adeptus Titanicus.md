# Adeptus Titanicus

> *"The ground shakes, and the sky burns."*

The Adeptus Titanicus (Collegia Titanica) in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition represents the Titan Legions — the god-machines of the Imperium. These towering war-engines ranging from Warhound Scouts to Imperator-class Titans are a Legendary/Lords of War support option rather than a standalone codex (primarily covered in *Legends* and *Adeptus Titanicus* the specialist game).
