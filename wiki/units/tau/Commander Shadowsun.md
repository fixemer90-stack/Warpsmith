## Lore Notes

Commander O'Shaserra — *Shadowsun*, the Supreme Commander of the T'au Empire's military forces. A master of stealth and tactical genius, she wears a custom XV22 stealth battlesuit and has faced the Imperium, Orks, Tyranids, and Necrons across countless campaigns. Once a student of Commander Farsight, she became one of the Empire's greatest heroes and its finest strategist.
