# Adepta Sororitas

> *"The Emperor protects."*

The Adepta Sororitas (Sisters of Battle) in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition are the militant arm of the Ecclesiarchy — all-female warrior orders clad in power armour, wielding boltguns, flamers, and the power of faith itself. They are a zealous, mid-to-high-elite army focused on aggressive mid-range engagements and Miracle Dice manipulation.

## Army Rule: Acts of Faith

Each battle round you generate Miracle Dice based on objectives held and actions performed. These can be substituted for any single roll (hit, wound, save, advance, charge, battle-shock) and can be manipulated by characters and stratagems.
