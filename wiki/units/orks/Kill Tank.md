# Kill Tank

*Legends datasheet*

Kill Tanks are the Ork answer to super-heavy tank design. Hulking, slab-sided machines, they sport a shovel-bladed prow for ramming aside obstacles and flimsier vehicles, and a wide-bore siege mortar that fires huge rocket-boosted ordnance.
