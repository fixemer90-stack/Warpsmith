# Squiggoth

*Legends datasheet*

Squiggoths are enormous scaly creatures bred as beasts of war by Ork Pigdoks. With armour panelling bolted to their already thick hides and crude howdahs built on their backs, they thunder into battle loaded with heavy weapons and Boyz.
