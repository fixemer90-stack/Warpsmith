# Secutarii Peltasts

> *"Skirmishers who screen the Titan Legions, the Secutarii Peltasts unleash punishing barrages from their galvanic casters."*

Secutarii Peltasts are the fire-support branch of the Titan Guard. Their galvanic casters can saturate an area with flechette fire or deliver concentrated hammershot blasts, while their Blind Barrage ability disrupts enemy aim.

## Abilities

### Faction: Doctrina Imperatives
This unit benefits from the Doctrina Imperatives army rule.

### Blind Barrage
After this unit has shot, select one enemy Infantry unit hit by one or more of those attacks. Until the end of the next phase, each time a model in that enemy unit makes a ranged attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
