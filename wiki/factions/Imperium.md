# Imperium of Man

> *"An open mind is like a fortress with its gates unbarred and unguarded."*

The Imperium of Man in Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition is the largest faction grouping in the game, encompassing humanity's war machine in all its forms. From the humble Guardsman to the superhuman Space Marine, from the ancient war machines of the Adeptus Titanicus to the shadowy operatives of the Imperial Agents, the Imperium fields the most diverse range of armies in the game.

## Overview

The Imperium is not a single army with a unified codex but a collection of distinct factions each with their own army rule, detachment options, and playstyle. Key mechanical themes across the Imperium include:

- **Faith and Leadership** (Adepta Sororitas, Grey Knights)
- **Elite Durability** (Adeptus Custodes, Space Marines)
- **Mechanical Augmentation** (Adeptus Mechanicus)
- **Massed Firepower** (Astra Militarum, Adeptus Titanicus)
- **Noble Houses** (Imperial Knights)
- **Covert Operations** (Imperial Agents)

## Army Rules by Faction

- **Adepta Sororitas** — Acts of Faith (Miracle Dice substituted for any roll)
- **Adeptus Custodes** — Martial Ka'tah (stance-switching combat stances)
- **Adeptus Mechanicus** — Doctrina Imperatives (toggling between Protective and Conqueror protocols)
- **Adeptus Titanicus** — Reanimation? Titan Fall?
- **Astra Militarum** — Born Soldiers (Lethal Hits on stationary turns, Sustained Hits 1 on ordered units)
- **Grey Knights** — Teleport Assault (deep strike and teleport shenanigans)
- **Imperial Agents** — Agents of the Imperium (rules for attached operatives)
- **Imperial Knights** — Oaths of the Noble Houses (Hekaton/Aegis/Lay Low the Tyrants)
- **Space Marines** — Oath of Moment (pick one enemy unit per battle round for full rerolls to hit and wound)
