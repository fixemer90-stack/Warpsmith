# Secutarii Hoplites

> *"The shield-bearing infantry of the Titan Legions, the Secutarii Hoplites advance in lockstep beneath a shimmering energy barrier."*

Secutarii Hoplites are the close-assault arm of the Titan Guard, wielding arc lances that crackle with disruptive energies. Their ion shields grant them a 4+ invulnerable save, making them remarkably durable for their size.

## Unit Composition

- 1 Secutarii Alpha
- 4–9 Secutarii Hoplites

The **Secutarii Alpha** is equipped with: arc lance; ion shield.

Every **Secutarii Hoplite** is equipped with: arc lance; ion shield.

The Secutarii Alpha can have its arc lance replaced with 1 archeotech pistol and 1 close combat weapon.

### Titan Guard
This unit is eligible to perform a Heroic Intervention as if it were a Monster or Vehicle. Each time a Charge roll is made for this unit, if that Charge targets a unit that is within 6\" of a friendly Adeptus Titanicus model, you can reroll that roll.
