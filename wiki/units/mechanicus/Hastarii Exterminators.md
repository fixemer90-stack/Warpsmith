# Hastarii Exterminators

> *"These heavily armoured Skitarii lay down withering firepower with heightened efficiency."*

The expanding energy cones of their eradication casters disrupt the target at a molecular level, grossly maiming or completely obliterating their victims. Combined with their shoulder-mounted arc blasters, this arsenal allows Hastarii Exterminators to blast the heart from the enemy battle line.
