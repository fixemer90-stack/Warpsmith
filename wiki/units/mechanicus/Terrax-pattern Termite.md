# Terrax-pattern Termite

> *"A burrowing assault transport deployed from orbit, the Terrax-pattern Termite bores through enemy lines with relentless fury."*

The Terrax-pattern Termite is a dedicated transport used by the Adeptus Mechanicus to deliver assault forces deep into enemy territory. Equipped with a massive drill and a variety of anti-armour weapons, it is as much a weapon as it is a transport.

## Abilities

### Faction: Doctrina Imperatives
This model benefits from the Doctrina Imperatives army rule.

### Deadly Demise D3
When this model is destroyed, roll one D6 before removing it from play. On a 6, it explodes and each unit within 6\" suffers D3 mortal wounds.

### Deep Strike
This model can be set up in Reserves instead of on the battlefield, and can be set up anywhere on the battlefield more than 9\" away from all enemy models.

### Termite Assault
This model must start the battle in Reserves. At the end of your Movement phase, you can set up this model anywhere on the battlefield more than 9\" away from any enemy models.
