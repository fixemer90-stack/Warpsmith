# Servitors

> *"Crudely augmented menials fitted with tool-arms and weapon mounts, Servitors follow their Tech-Priordinator's every command without question."*

Servitors are lobotomised labour-servants pressed into military service. Though individually fragile, their servo-arms and heavy weapons can be surprisingly effective when overseen by a Tech-Priest Enginseer.

## Servitor Retinue

When you declare a unit that contains a Tech-Priest Enginseer model as a unit making a Leader attachment at the start of the Declare Battle Formations step, this unit can join that unit. If it does, the Servitors are attached to that unit and are not counted towards the limit of one Character model per unit. Each model in this unit is treated as having a Wounds characteristic of 1.

## Abilities

### Faction: Doctrina Imperatives
This unit benefits from the Doctrina Imperatives army rule.

### Mindlock
While this unit is within 6\" of a friendly Tech-Priest model, add 1 to the Ballistic Skill and Weapon Skill characteristics of this unit's weapons.
