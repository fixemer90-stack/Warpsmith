## Abilities

**CORE:** Deadly Demise D6+2, Firing Deck 22

**FACTION:** Waaagh!

- **Rolling Fortress:** Each time a ranged attack is allocated to an Orks model from your army, if that model is not fully visible to every model in the attacking unit because of this Deff Rolla Battle Fortress model, that model has the Benefit of Cover against that attack.

**DAMAGED: 1-7 WOUNDS REMAINING:** While this model has 1-7 wounds remaining, subtract 4 from its Objective Control characteristic and each time this model makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
